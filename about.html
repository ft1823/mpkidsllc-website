About MP Kids

Creating a nurturing environment where children can learn, grow, and thrive

Our Story

MP Kids was founded with a simple mission: to provide a safe, loving, and stimulating environment where children can develop their full potential. Located in the vibrant community of Borough Park, Brooklyn, we understand the unique needs of families in our neighborhood.

Our family day care combines the warmth of a home setting with the structure and activities of a quality early childhood program. We believe that every child deserves individual attention, creative expression, and the opportunity to learn through play.
